Alec Lau's Website

For a while I've thought that having a website to share some stuff useful to people including me would be a good idea, so here it is.

I am currently a multiphysics specialist at Tesla. I like tinkering, math, and physics (I do all three at Tesla), and have posted some of my old (non-professional) pet projects here. I also do casual pure mathematics, and my main interests are in topology, algebra, and theoretical condensed matter physics. I'm also interested in pretty much any math related to the name "Witten," and have kept a repository of some references of math I've done. In the rest of my spare time I like drawing, taekwondo, and thinking about movies, so I put a list of my all-time favorite movies on here, as well as my recent drawings.
